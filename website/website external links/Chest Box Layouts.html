CHESTBOX LAYOUTS

by Michael Gallant

These are designed to be guides for the placement of components and are not screen/prop accurate.
  Revenge of the Sith

Note that the dimensions given are incorrect. The ROTS box is 6 1/8" square, not 6 3/4" square. The placement is accurate at the scale given.

Each rocker switch stands alone. There are lights in the coin slots, square colored panels, and red rocker switch. No brackets at the top or bottom of side rods. No silver switches.

  A New Hope

Each rocker switch stands alone. There are NO lights on the ANH box at all. No brackets at the top or bottom of side rods. No silver switches.

  Empire Strikes Back

The rocker switches are part of a single construction, with only the faces of the "switches" coloured. Note the one larger bracket on top of the left side rod. There are silver switches between all rockers, and over each colored Only coin slots light up.

The Arubesh text is not shown because there is some debate regarding what is accurate.

 
  Return of the Jedi

Again, a single construction for the switches, like ESB. Note the inverted black brackets on the top of the chestrods, and they are both same size now. There is no silver switch in middle rocker switch position, and no silver switch over top colored panel. Only the coin slots light up. Although it is not shown on the illustration, the RotJ coin slots have black on the forward facing edge.

The Arubesh text is not shown because there is some debate regarding what is accurate.
